Introducing FOOCA Camera

The Essential NFT for Contributing and Earning on Fooday

Ready to become a top contributor on Fooday? You'll need a Fooca Camera.

This essential NFT serves as proof of your dedication to the platform and allows you to earn FUDOS for your contributions. Fooca Camera is the key to unlocking exclusive rewards and elevating your status within the Fooday community.

Fooca Camera is a unique NFT that has four attributes: Computing, Quality, Battery, and Luck. These attributes affect the performance of the FUDOS that you earn when contributing to the Fooday platform.

Computing

Computing is related to the amount of Energy points that users consume when performing a contribution action on Fooday. The more Computing score the Fooca has, the less Energy points the user will consume when performing such an action.

Quality

Quality determines the amount of FUDOS that users can earn per contribution. The higher the Quality score of the Fooca, the more FUDOS the user can collect per contribution.

Battery

The Battery attribute of a Fooca NFT determines how many times it can be used before needing to be recharged. A higher Battery attribute means that the Fooca Camera can be used more times before it becomes unusable. The Battery attribute does not affect a Foodie‚Äôs energy level, but a low Battery attribute can decrease the efficiency of each action, making it earn fewer Fudos.

Luck

Luck, is related to the chance of users getting a loot box when they equip their Fooca to contribute. The higher the Luck score of the Fooca, the higher the chance the user will get a loot box, which can contain rewards such as additional FUDOS or other rare items.
